Sugar Donuts

Description

A chewy, sugar covered dough treat fit for any occassion or craving.

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 Teaspoons Of Active Dry Yeast
  • 2 Tablespoons Of Warm Water
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Of Granluated Sugar
  • 3 and 1/4 Cups Of All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Ounces Of Unsalted Butter
  • 3 Large Egg Yolks
  • 2 Tablespoons Of Granulated Sugar
  • 1/4 Teaspoons Of Kosher Salt
  • 1 Cup of Granulated Sugar

Cooking Steps

  1. Stir 2 tbsp warm water, 1/2 tsp sugar & 2 1/2 tsp active dry yeast in tall glass. Leave to rise for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Mix 3 1/4 cups of flour, 1 cup of warm milk, 2 oz room temperature butter, 3 yolks, 2 tbsp suggar, 1/4 tsp of salt, and proofed yeast mixture in mixer on low speed with dough hook until dough comes together. slightly increase speed and knead for another 15-20 minutes.
  3. Pick up dough and form into a ball. Butter a large bowl, place dough ball into bowl then butter dough itself.
  4. Cover with clean kitchen towel and allow to rise for 1-2 hours.
  5. Cut out 5x5 squares of parchment wax paper
  6. Punch down dough and turn onto greased surface. Roll into 1/2 inches of thickness and cut as many rounds as possible with 3 inch round cookie cutter. place each dough disk onto piece of parchment.
  7. Cover cut doughnuts with clean kitchen towel and and allow to rise for 45 minutes to an hours.
  8. Heat 1 and 1/2 inches of oil in a heavy pot to 350Â°F. Fry donuts several at a time until golden on one side before turning to other.
  9. Transfer to paper towel or wire rack and allow to cool.
  10. Once doughntus are cooled, roll in granulated sugar to coat.
