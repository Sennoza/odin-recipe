Cheesecake

Description

A sweet and creamy desert with a flexible palette and buttery crunch. This cream cheese delight is supported by a graham cracker crust, allowing you to put any kind of sweet, savory, or salty topping on it such as a strawberry sauce or Lemon Curd.

Ingredients

  • Four 8-ounce Cream Cheese Blocks
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Cup of Sour Cream
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Teaspoons of Lemon Juice
  • 3 Eggs
