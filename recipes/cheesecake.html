Cheesecake

Description

A sweet and creamy desert with a flexible palette and buttery crunch. This cream cheese delight is supported by a graham cracker crust, allowing you to put any kind of sweet, savory, or salty topping on it such as a strawberry sauce or Lemon Curd.

Ingredients

- Crust
  • 11/2 Cups of Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 1/4 Cup of Confectioners' Sugar
  • 6 Tablespoons of Melted Butter
  • 1/8 Teaspoon of Salt
- Filling
  • Two 8-ounce packages of Creeam Cheese, Room Temperature
  • 2/3 Cup of Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs, Room Temperature
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract

Cooking Steps

  1. Use pie pan with height of 11/4" and with a top dimension of 9". Preheat in oven to 350°F.
  2. Stir together all crust ingredients in mixing bowl until well combined.
  3. Press crumb mixture into the bottom and sides of pie pan.
  4. In another mixing bowl, mix cream cheese and and sugar until smooth then mix in eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Set pie pan onto baking sheet and poor filling into crust.
  6. Place baking sheet into oven and bake for 20 minutes.
  7. Add aluminum foil around outside of pan to shield crust, bake for additional 30 minutes. Insert thermometer into filling, thermometer should read between 160°F and 170°F
  8. Remove cheesecake from oven and set on cooling rack. Once cool place into refrigerator covered until ready to server.
  9. Serve cheesecake in wedges with any additional toppings.
