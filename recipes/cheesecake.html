Cheesecake

Description

A sweet and creamy desert with a flexible palette and buttery crunch. This cream cheese delight is supported by a graham cracker crust, allowing you to put any kind of sweet, savory, or salty topping on it such as a strawberry sauce or Lemon Curd.

Ingredients

- Crust
  • 11/2 Cups of Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 1/4 Cup of Confectioners' Sugar
  • 6 Tablespoons of Melted Butter
  • 1/8 Teaspoon of Salt
- Filling
  • Two 8-ounce packages of Creeam Cheese, Room Temperature
  • 2/3 Cup of Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs, Room Temperature
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
