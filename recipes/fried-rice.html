Fried Rice

Description

A classic, savory and inexpensive dish made with leftover rice, eggs, and fresh vegetables

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Cup of Chopped Baby Carrots
  • 1/2 Cup of Frozen Green Peas
  • 2 Tablespoons of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Clove of Garlic, Minced
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 3 Cups of Lefover White Rice
  • 1 Tablespoon of Soy Sauce
  • 2 Teaspoons Of Sesame Oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Assemble ingredients
  2. Place carrots in small sauce pan, cover with water and bring to a low boil for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas then drain in colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat, pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic. Cook for about 30 seconds, add eggs and stir quickly to scramble.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve on dish
